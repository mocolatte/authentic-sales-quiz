Discover Your Sales Archetype




1. When preparing for a sales conversation, you typically:

Focus solely on what the client needs, even at the expense of your own boundaries.
Have a great idea but struggle to clearly articulate it.
Get caught up in over-explaining, feeling like you need to prove your value.
Confidently position your offer as a transformative solution.

2. How do you feel when discussing pricing?

Anxious and worried they’ll think it’s too expensive.
Unsure and avoid giving a firm number.
Nervous, so you often discount or add bonuses to ‘sweeten the deal.’
Clear and confident, knowing the value is worth every cent.

3. When a prospect says 'I need to think about it,' your response is to:

Apologize and ask what you could have done differently.
Avoid pushing, fearing they’ll ghost you.
Offer a discount to try to close the deal quickly.
Reframe the objection and clarify their hesitations.

4. What’s your biggest challenge in sales right now?

Overgiving and undervaluing your time.
Lacking clarity in your messaging.
Being too pushy or desperate to close.
Consistently attracting aligned clients.

5. How do you feel after a sales call?

Drained and second-guessing yourself.
Confused about what went wrong.
Frustrated that they didn’t say yes.
Energized and proud of the clarity you brought to the conversation.

6. What’s your current approach to follow-up?

You keep giving free advice to stay top of mind.
You’re hesitant to follow up, feeling like you’re annoying them.
You try to add bonuses to get them to say yes.
You follow up with a clear invitation and firm boundaries.

7. What’s your biggest fear when it comes to selling?

Being seen as too aggressive or salesy.
No one understanding the value of your offer.
Hearing 'no' and feeling like a failure.
Not being seen as a leader in your field.

8. What would you most like to change about your sales process?

Stop overgiving and undervaluing yourself.
Get clear on what your offer really is.
Feel more confident without being pushy.
Align with clients who are a clear yes and ready to buy.
